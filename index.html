We get our colors from a hashtag called a hex color code. These codes are used by programmers to color aspects of a webpage or website. You can go to this link to choose the color you would like. All you have to do is type in a color in the search bar and write down the corresponding numbers or letters. This hashtag is the color you want your lightbulb to be not your actual light.
